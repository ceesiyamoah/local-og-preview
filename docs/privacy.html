What we collect

Nothing. OG Link Preview collects no personal information, no usage data, and no analytics of any kind.

The extension reads Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags from the currently active browser tab when you click the extension icon. This data is used only to display previews and validation results within the popup. It is never stored, logged, or sent anywhere.
